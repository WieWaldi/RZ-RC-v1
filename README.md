## Motivation
How to make a long range remote control with 100mW NRF24L01 PA LN (E01-ML01DP5)
2.4Ghz module and Arduino. 1 to 6 proportional channels and 2 ON/OFF type
channels. Circuit construction with a total of 8 channels.
